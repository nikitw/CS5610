Sliding Menu

The sliding menu is similar to the dropdown menu which uses ul and li combinations to generate the menu. The only difference is this uses a lot of transition features in CSS and most of the modern browsers support them today. The position of nav as observed is fixed and it slides to the right as you hover over the belt on left of the screen It is also observed that the body of the page slides to the left to accomodate the nav bar.
