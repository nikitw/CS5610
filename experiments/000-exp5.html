Grid layout (960px) (12 cells)

The grid is basically an alternative to table generated using CSS. The following grid spans over 960px centered container. Choice of 960px justifies its divisibility by 12 at granular level.

Combination of differently sized granules can act as a building block needed for generating a layout as needed. The below is just a simple representation of how differently sized cells make up a neat layout.
